mocha brownies

recipe by chef zarnak sidhwa

ingredients

  • flour 1/2 cup

  • salt 1/4 tsp

  • baking powder 1/4 tsp

  • cocoa powder 1-1/2 tsp

  • butter 4 tsp

  • vanilla assence 1/2 tsp

  • chopped choclate 1 cup

  • coffee powder

  • eggs 2

  • brown sugar 3/4 cup

cooking directions

  1. pre heat oven to 180 degree c.

  2. grease and flour an 8" square pan and line with paper and greased it well too.

  3. in a bowl, add flour, cocoa powder, salt and baking powder.

  4. mix and keep aside.

  5. place a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water.

  6. place butter in a bowl and let it melt.

  7. remove the bowl from the simmering water and stirr in the coffee
    powder and chopped choclate and stirr it till melts
    completely.

  8. whisk an egg and add the vanilla assence before adding the sugar.

  9. mix well.

  10. pour butter into prepared pen.

  11. bake in pre heated over at 180 degrees c for 35-40 mins until a
    tootpick inserted in centre comes out with a few moist crumbs
    attached.

  12. cool on a wire rack for 30 minutes.

  13. cut into squares.

  14. stores in an air tight container
